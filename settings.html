FAQ

Regular Breaks is a feature that will remind you to take a break from your work at regular intervals. The frequency of these reminders is customizable and can be set in minutes.

Mindfulness Reminders will periodically remind you to take a moment and center your thoughts, promoting a more mindful and focused work environment.

The 'Positive Affirmation Reminders' feature provides you with periodic positive affirmations to boost your mood and morale. The frequency of these reminders can be set to your preference.

The 'Pomodoro Technique Reminders' feature helps you manage your time effectively using the Pomodoro Technique. This technique uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. You will receive reminders to start a new Pomodoro session and to take breaks after each session.

The 'Social Interaction Reminders' feature prompts you to engage in social activities periodically. In a work-from-home setting, it's easy to become isolated. This feature will remind you to interact with others, improving your social well-being.

The 'Healthy Habit Reminders' feature encourages you to engage in healthy habits periodically. This can range from reminding you to drink water to suggesting a quick exercise. It's customizable to match your personal health goals.
